Hello CS50 world!

My name is Kiko

I am from Bulgaria and I'm taking CS50 as the beginning of my journey of hopefully becoming a software developer.

Here is a short story about my life.

In Bulgaria I grew up in a medium-size town called Asenovgrad. When I was in high school I thought I wanted to be a civil engineer. So when I graduated I went off to Sofia to study that in university. But after the first year I already had changed my mind. Living in Sofia I started cooking for myself and really enjoyed it. Also being a student I had the chance to go to USA for a Work and Travel program. Twice. That way I discovered my love for travelling. And so it made sense to me that becoming a chef it would also be easier for me to travel from time to time. And that is what I did. Since then I've visited more than 20 countries, worked in 4 different countries and in 2021 I started my own small business in my hometown Asenovgrad. The end of this summer I sold my business and now I am headed in a totaly different direction, exploring my new pasion - Programming!
